How to Use the Bus - Step by Step

1

Find Your Route & Stop

Use Google Maps or Tainan Bus App to find your route (15, 21, 5, 88, 99, etc.). Locate the correct bus stop with route number display. Near STUST: Chi Mei Medical Center, campus gates, or Yongkang area stops.

2

Confirm Your Destination Stop

Use the app to see ALL stops on your route. Note the exact stop name where you need to get off. Example: "Bus 15 from STUST to Tainan Train Station (South Station)".

3

Wave to the Driver

Stand clearly visible at the bus stop. Wave or signal to the driver as the bus approaches. The driver will stop if you flag them.

4

Pay the Fare

Pay the driver: NT$20-35 depending on distance. Have exact coins ready. OR tap your iPass/EasyCard on the reader by the driver.

5

Watch for Your Stop

Watch the app or ask nearby passengers for your stop. Listen for announcements (Chinese/English on newer buses).

6

Exit at Correct Stop

Press the bell/button near the door BEFORE your stop. Wait for the bus to completely stop. Exit safely. Tap your card again if you paid with card.

üö¥ YouBike - Eco-Friendly Bike Rental

üö¥ What is YouBike?

YouBike is Taiwan's bike-sharing system with stations throughout Tainan. Perfect for short-distance travel, exploring the city at your own pace, and staying eco-friendly!

How to Rent a YouBike

1

Download the YouBike App

Download "YouBike 2.0" from iOS App Store or Google Play Store. Register using email address.

2

Add Payment Method

Use credit card (VISA, MasterCard, JCB) or EasyCard/iPass. Credit card requires NT$3,000 pre-authorization deposit (refunded later).

3

Find a Station

Use the app to locate the nearest YouBike station. Stations are throughout downtown Tainan and tourist areas.

4

Unlock & Ride

Scan the QR code on the bike or tap your EasyCard. The lock releases. Enjoy your ride!

5

Return & Lock

Return to any YouBike station, place bike in empty dock. Lock automatically engages. You're charged automatically.
